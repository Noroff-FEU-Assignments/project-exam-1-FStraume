My Journey

As I look back on my life, I see a journey that has been intimately intertwined with the cold embrace of snow and the thrill of gravity-defying descents. From a young age, I was drawn to the mountains and the world of skiing, embarking on a lifelong adventure that has left an indelible mark on my soul. My love for skiing began when I was just a child. I still remember the excitement that welled up inside me as I stood atop a snowy slope for the first time, wearing boots that felt twice as big as my feet and skis that were almost as tall as I was. I took that initial wobbly glide down the hill, and from that moment, I was hooked. The sensation of the wind in my face, the crisp mountain air, and the pure joy of sliding on snow was like nothing I had ever experienced. My journey as a skier was not always smooth. There were tumbles, crashes, and countless times I had to pick myself up and try again. Yet, those challenges only fueled my determination. I spent winters honing my skills, perfecting my turns, and gradually progressing from the bunny slopes to the steeper and more challenging runs.

Fredrik Straume

Founder & Creator of The PowderChase
